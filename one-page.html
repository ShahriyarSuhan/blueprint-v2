[ HOW IT WORKS ]

SIMPLE, REPEATABLE, WHITE-LABEL WORKFLOW

From brief to live site —
in three simple steps.

  • STEP 01

    Submit a brief

    Fill a short intake form with business name, goal, logo, brand colors, selected template (if any), and content. The clearer the brief, the faster we deliver.

  • STEP 02

    Design & build

    We pick the matching template, swap branding, add content, and implement forms or integrations. You receive a preview link (staging) for review — usually within 12–48 hours.

  • STEP 03

    Review & launch

    Request one revision (included). After approval we connect the domain, enable SSL, and publish the site. Handover files and deployment details are provided — white-labeled under your agency.

White-label process: you present, we build — fast, reliable, and delivered under your brand.

[ FAQS ]

ANSWERS TO YOUR CLIENTS' TOP QUESTIONS.

Got questions?
We’ve got clear answers.

What packages do you offer?

Starter ($25), Standard ($35), and Premium ($45) landing pages — plus WooCommerce setups starting at $80. Each tier lists what’s included so clients can pick the best fit.

How long does a landing page take?

Typical delivery for landing pages is 12–48 hours (Starter), 2–4 days (Standard), and 4–5 days (Premium) depending on complexity and content readiness.

Do you work white-label for agencies?

Yes — all work is white-labeled. Sites are delivered under your agency’s name; you handle client communication and pricing, I handle development and deployment.

What do you need from the client to start?

A short intake form: business name, logo, brand colors, main headline, short description, images (if any), chosen template (optional), and domain details if publishing is required.

How many revisions are included?

One round of revisions is included with each package. Additional revisions or custom feature requests can be added as paid add-ons.

Do you handle domain, SSL, and publishing?

Yes — domain linking, SSL, and deployment to Vercel/Netlify or custom hosts are included with Standard and Premium packages. Starter can be delivered as a ZIP or staging link.

What about WooCommerce or bigger projects?

WooCommerce setups start at $80 (base). For larger e-commerce projects we provide a custom quote based on product count, integrations, and complexity.

How do payments and bulk orders work?

For single landing pages we accept per-project payments. For bulk or recurring work we can invoice weekly or biweekly. For larger builds (WooCommerce), a 50% upfront deposit is recommended.

White-label process: you present — we build fast, reliable, and brandable websites for your clients.
